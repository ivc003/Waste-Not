# Waste-Not
A hackathon project forecasting and providing solutions for urban food waste.

Food waste as a market failure

information asymmetry: corresponding sides are unaware of their  options—stores don't know who needs near-expiry inventory. Buyers don't know what's available.

Build the information junction to correct the market..
